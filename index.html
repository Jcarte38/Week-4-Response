Roland Barthes’ “Rhetoric of the Image” is a very detailed scholarly esoteric analysis of the language of images in photography.
His style of communication and sharing information is obtuse, which is to say that it is indistinct and not easy to perceive.
The three messages he mentions in his article are:

1. Linguistic message- which describes the elements of a scene through language.
2. A coded iconic message
3. Non coded conic message

The other two messages are not clearly defined in his lengthy article therefore it was inopportune to provide an example of them.
